Importing Data 8

Raw data Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/transport#carbon-intensity-of-new-passenger-vehicles

The dataset has 3 tables (I have downloaded data from first chart and last 2 charts)
  1. Save those files on your device.
  2. Open Power BI Desktop.
  3. Click on the Get Data button.
  4. Select Excel workbook, choose new-vehicles-type-number-table from the downloaded files and click open.
  5. A preview of data will be displayed, select the table, Click on Transform data and the data is loaded into power query editor.
