CO2 (Filled Map - Bottom Right) & Legend 24

  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Filled map.
  2. Fill up fields: Location=country, Tooltips=new vehicle EV QTY YOY %,new vehicle EV share YOY%, Renew Consumption YoY %, new vehicles CO2_per_km YOY %.
  3. Rename for the visual: new vehicle EV QTY YOY %->EV QTY YoY, new vehicle EV share YOY%=EV share YoY, Renew Consumption YoY %=Renew Consumption YoY,  new vehicles CO2_per_km YOY %=CO2 YoY.
  4. For Format  visual: Map settings: style= grayscale, show labels=on, controls: auto zoom= on, zoom button= on. fill colors: use below screen shot (colors for rule1=#EAE259, rule2=#EEAD3C, rule 3=#D08414, rule4=#E57747, rule5=#DE4A0A, rule 6=#FF0303.

5. For Format  General: Properties, Size, Height=320, Width=539, Position, Horizontal= 741, Vertical=400. Title=On, Text=CO2, Font=DIN (15), Text color= #FFFFFF, Background color=#59D8E6 , Horizontal alignment=Center
6. To do the map legend, go to insert tab, click on image and select the Map_Legend and click open.
7. In the format pane, go to general and enter the bellow properties: Height=30, width=99, horizontal=857, vertical=660, title=off.
8. To do the map legend text indicator, go to the insert tab, click on text box, click inside the box and enter 'HIGH  LOW', font=segoe UI, bold(8), Left aligned , color=black
9. In the format pane, go to general: Properties: Height=32, width=98, horizontal=858,vertical=644, title=off, effects-background=off.
