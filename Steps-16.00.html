Year Slicer 16

1. Click on canvas, click slicer in the visualizations and drag year from calendar table to values field.
2. In the format->visual section, set slicer header off, values ->click Bold(leave other settings to default), slider color->black.
3.  In the format->general section, set
Height=81
width=194
horizontal=802
vertical=0
title=off
effects-background=on, transparency=0%
