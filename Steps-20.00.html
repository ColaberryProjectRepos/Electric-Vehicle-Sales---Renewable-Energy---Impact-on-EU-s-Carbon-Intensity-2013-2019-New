Year (Line and Clustered Column Chart - Top Left) 20

  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Line and Clustered Column Chart.
  2. Fill up fields X- axis = Year; Column Y-Axis = battery_electric_number; Line Y-Axis =new vehicle EV QTY YOY %,new vehicle EV share YOY%, Renew Consumption YoY %, new vehicles CO2_per_km YOY % ; Tooltips = new vehicle EV QTY YOY %,new vehicle EV share YOY%, Renew Consumption YoY %, new vehicles CO2_per_km YOY %.
  3. rename for the visual (Line y-axis): new vehicle EV QTY YOY %->EV QTY YoY, new vehicle EV share YOY%=EV share YoY, Renew Consumption YoY %=Renew Consumption YoY, new vehicles CO2_per_km YOY %=CO2 YoY. column y-axis: EV Qty.
  4. In Format Visual: X-axis: Type = Continuous, Values: Font=Segoe UI (9),color=black, Title - off  Y-axis: off. Secondary Y-axis: Values: Font=Segoe UI (9).  Legend: Position- Top left, Style Marker (Circle default), Text: Font Segoe UI(10), Gridlines: off, Color= Columns: Colors= #155085. Lines: line style: solid, join type: round, stroke width: 4px, Colors: CO2YoY=#D86D10, Renew Consumption YoY=#A562C9, EV Share YoY=#20A49E, EV QTY YoY=#118DFF.Markers:on. Data Labels: on , series=EV QTY, show data labels=on, position =inside end ,values: font=segoe UI(9), color= white, background=off(for rest of the series data label off) 
  5. For Format  General: Properties, Size, Height=320, Width=540, Position, Horizontal= 200, Vertical=80. Title=On, Text=Year, Font=DIN (15), Text color= #FFFFFF, Background color=#59D8E6 , Horizontal alignment=Center, Effects: Background=On, Background color= #E6E6E6, Transparency=20%.
